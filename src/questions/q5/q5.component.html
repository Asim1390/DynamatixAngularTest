Q5 Managing your treatments

This means:

  • Monitoring changes in your health condition or disability (for example, your blood sugar level, mental state, or pain levels)
  • Taking medication in the right way and at the right time
  • Managing therapies that take place at home which have been recommended by a health professional or pharmacist (for example, physiotherapy or home dialysis)
